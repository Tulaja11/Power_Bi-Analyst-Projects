# Disha Ecommerce Sales Dashboard
An interactive sales analytics dashboard built in Power BI to visualize ecommerce performance across customers, categories, states, and payment modes.
---

---
- KPIs: Total Amount (438K), Quantity (5615), Profit (37K), and Average Order Value
- Charts: Sales by State, Category, Sub-Category, Customer, Payment Mode, and monthly Profit trends
- Filters: Quarter (Qtr 1–4) and Category selector for interactive drill-down
---
## Data Sources
- FileDescriptionOrders.csvOrder ID, Date, Customer Name, State, CityDetails.csvOrder ID, Amount, Profit, Quantity, Category, Sub-Category, Payment Mode
---
## Key Insights
- Clothing dominates sales at 63% of quantity
- Maharashtra leads in revenue by state
- COD is the most popular payment mode (44%)
- Printers are the highest profit sub-category
